Three Dice Game
Created: 04/16/2020
You are given a dice and must choose to roll again (up to 3 rolls) or keep your roll.

You are given a 6 sided dice. After you roll you get the amount back in dollars (1 = $1, 2 = $2,...).
After you roll once you can keep or roll again. After your second roll you can keep
or roll again. After your third roll you must keep the roll.
